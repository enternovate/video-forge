# Security Policy

## Our Commitment

Video Forge processes all media **locally on your device**. No data is ever transmitted to any server.

- **No telemetry** — we do not collect usage data, crash reports, or analytics
- **No accounts** — no sign-up, no login, no user tracking
- **No AI** — no cloud services, no data sent to external APIs
- **Royalty-free codecs** — only VP8/VP9/AV1 codecs are bundled. No H.264/H.265/MPEG-LA patented formats
- **Open source** — fully auditable under MIT license

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Open a GitHub issue or email security@enternovate.co.za. Response within 48 hours.
